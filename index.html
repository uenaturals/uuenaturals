How to Use uenaturals

Simple Steps for Maximum Results

1

Apply to Scalp

Apply a small amount of oil directly to the scalp and hair roots for targeted nourishment.

2

Gentle Massage

Gently massage your scalp for 5–10 minutes using circular motions to boost circulation and absorption.

3

Let It Sit

Leave the oil on for a few hours or overnight to let the nutrients deeply penetrate and nourish your hair.

4

Wash with Shampoo

Wash your hair thoroughly with a mild shampoo to remove the oil and reveal soft, shiny, nourished hair.

5

Consistent Use

Use 2–3 times per week for best results. Consistency is key to achieving your hair transformation goals.

Pro Tips for Best Results:

  • Warm the oil slightly before application for better absorption
  • Focus on the scalp rather than the ends for hair growth benefits
  • Use a shower cap to retain moisture and heat while the oil works
  • Be patient – visible results typically appear within 4-6 weeks
